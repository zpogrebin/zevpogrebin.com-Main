About Me

Zev Pogrebin is a third year electrical engineer at Tufts University. He enjoys making things, whether circuits, tangible objects, designs, sounds, or art. After tinkering with electronics and computers as a child, his interest in EE intensified during high school when he interned for a recording engineer. Zev became curious about how all the equipment—the amplifiers, filters, and other systems—worked. As Zev delves into these designs, the intersection between art and engineering continues to facinate him. In his spare time, he has found himself learning about type design, pattern making in fashion, film photography, and book arts.
