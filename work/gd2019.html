Kind Thing; Nice Thing

A campaign for a play's summer reading

Kind Thing; Nice Thing is "a two-act non-linear play about art, heartbreak, family, and mermaids" written by Megan Rivkin. The play includes gender non binary roles. I was brought on to create graphics to promote and support the production. The project included posters, digital graphics for a social media campaign, an html email template, and a webpage.
