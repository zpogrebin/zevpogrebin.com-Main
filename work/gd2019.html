Kind Thing; Nice Thing

A campaign for a play's summer reading

Kind Thing; Nice Thing is "a two-act non-linear play about art, heartbreak, family, and mermaids" written by Megan Rivkin. The play includes gender non binary roles. I was brought on to create graphics to promote and support the production. The project included posters, digital graphics for a social media campaign, an html email template, and a webpage.

Untrue To Form

Creating a new image for a student theater group

After changing their name from Bare Bodkin to Untrue to Form (UTF), Tufts's experimental theater group wanted to update their logo, color scheme, and other brand assets. I worked alongside the group's media archivist to decide upon a consistent design language and develop rough ideas into final designs.

'Sound Play'

Designing for an immersive theater experience

This project involved the creation of posters, digital promotions, and other materials for a sound oriented immersive theater project. I used imagery of waves to tie into the technical qualities that were central to the production, and color gradients that related to the script's themes.

Orientation Show Poster

Poster design for 'o'shows' from Tufts Theater organizations

I was tasked with creating a poster to advertise performances of You're a Good Man, Charlie Brown and The Realistic Joneses. The two productions were very thematically different. I opted to create a poster design that individualizes both shows through their graphics' color and style while creating a consistent framework of typography, composition, and space to tie both pieces together.
